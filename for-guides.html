How Judhur Works

We provide two flexible models for you to connect with travelers and earn on your terms.

For Individual Guides

The Proposal Model

Showcase your personal expertise and connect directly with travelers seeking authentic, customized experiences.

  • Create Your Profile: Highlight your skills, languages, and unique tour offerings.
  • Bid on Custom Trips: Browse traveler requests and submit proposals for tours that match your expertise.
  • Get Hired & Paid: Communicate directly, get hired, and receive secure payments through our platform.

For Tour Companies

The Fixed Experience Model

List your pre-packaged tours and reach a wider audience of travelers looking for expertly crafted itineraries.

  • Build Your Company Page: Showcase your brand, your team of guides, and your full range of tour packages.
  • List Fixed Tours: Publish your curated experiences with set prices, itineraries, and dates for direct booking.
  • Manage Bookings: Receive bookings directly and manage your customer relations through our dashboard.
