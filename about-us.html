Planting Journeys in Authentic Roots

We are Judhur (جذور), a team dedicated to bridging the gap between travelers and the true cultural heart of Egypt.

Our Story

Judhur, meaning "Roots," was born from a simple observation: travelers crave authentic local experiences, yet the people who can provide them—the local guides, artisans, and communities—are often disconnected from the global audience.

We saw that guides and local providers were losing significant portions of their income to intermediaries, while travelers were missing out on deep, meaningful cultural connections. At the same time, rural communities were seeing their youth leave for cities in search of opportunities.

Our solution is a platform built on fairness and connection. We empower local experts by giving them a direct channel to showcase their skills and stories, ensuring they receive fair compensation. For travelers, we unlock a world of genuine experiences, from customized adventures with a local guide to handcrafted goods from a village artisan, all while supporting the sustainability of local communities.

Our Mission

We are committed to creating a more equitable and authentic tourism model.

Empower Locals

Provide guides and artisans with the tools to build their own businesses and earn a fair wage.

Foster Authenticity

Connect travelers with genuine cultural experiences that go beyond the typical tourist trail.

Support Communities

Promote sustainable tourism that directly benefits and helps preserve local communities and villages.
