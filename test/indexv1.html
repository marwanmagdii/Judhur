Connect tourists with authentic Egyptian experiences through our three-way platform

Tourists

  • ●Choose from pre-made tours by companies

  • ●Receive bids from local tour guides
  • ●Interview and choose your perfect guide

Tour Guides

Share your local expertise

  • ●Browse custom trip requests
  • ●Submit competitive bids with your rates

Tourism Companies

The Journey Process

1. Discover

Tourist explores options or creates custom trip request.

2. Connect

Guides bid on custom trips or companies receive applications.

3. Interview

Tourist interviews potential guides to find the perfect match.

4. Experience

Enjoy an authentic Egyptian adventure with your chosen guide.
